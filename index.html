Photography

Definition

Photography is basically a process to capture light using a device called camera and making image electronically or by chemically using light-sensitive material.

Different Types of Photography

  • Lifestyle
  • Documentary
  • Traditional or Posed
  • Artistic

Lifestyle

Lifestyle photography is just like the same way as it sounds.

Documentary

Documentary photography is based on some chronological events.

Traditional or Posed

Traditional or posed photography is the common portrait styles.

Artistic

Artistic photography allows both the photographer and the subject the most freedom. They work as a team to create a piece of art with no guidelines or rules to follow.
